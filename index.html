About this directory

Child Care Finder Australia is a simple, free directory highlighting family day care and home-based child care services across Australia. This first version contains sample listings so you can see the layout — you can replace these with real providers when ready.

Available family day care listings

How to use

  1. Search by name or suburb, or filter by state.
  2. Click the contact link to email or call the provider.
  3. Providers: use the "List your service" button to email your details for inclusion.

Provider quick tips

  • Include clear suburb and service hours.
  • Share qualifications and any special programs (ECE, language, cultural focus).
  • Keep descriptions brief — parents skim listings.

Add your service

Click "List your service" to open an email form pre-filled with the subject and body so you can send your details (no login required).
